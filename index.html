About Me

I am a web/print designer who works with a network of other designers, developers and partner companies. I work with clients to produce bespoke, fully considered, responsive websites that translate the needs and requirements of both clients and website visitors. As a frontend designer I put an emphasis on form and function, to create an enjoyable user experience. This means establishing the look and feel of web pages, including typography, graphics, colour, layout and branding.

I develop websites using HTML5, CSS3, PHP, Wordpress and the Magento e-commerce platform. With my partners I am able to design and develop websites to suit most client budgets from simple business/personal sites to full e-commerce solutions.

So why choose me over a design agency?

The main benefits of using a freelance designer is that you will often get the same standard of work but for a fraction of the price. You will also be able to work closely and share ideas much more easily on a more personal level. I always make sure I am easy to contact and provide a unique level of service, which can be hard to come by when working with a design agency.

To find out more about what I can do for you contact me at studio@nickgelardi.com.
